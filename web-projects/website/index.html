Manchester United

Manchester United is an English football team. Manchester United is one of the greatest teams in the world with world class players.

Manchester United have won many cups over the years. These cups are as follows:

  • FA Charity/Community shield
  • FA Premier League/Football League Division One
  • FA Cup
  • Football League Cup
  • UEFA Champions League
